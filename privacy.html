Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to BookTracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our book tracking application.

Information We Collect

  • Email address - Provided through Google Sign-In authentication
  • Name - From your Google profile (optional, can be customized)
  • Book data - Books you add to your library including titles, authors, ratings, reviews, reading status, and dates

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the BookTracker service
  • To authenticate your account securely
  • To store and display your personal book collection
  • To enable features like filtering and organizing your library

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database with industry-standard encryption)
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes
  • We do not sell your information

Third-Party Services

BookTracker uses the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth - For secure authentication (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • OpenLibrary API - To fetch book covers and author information (no personal data is sent)
  • Supabase - For secure database hosting and authentication services

Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data within the application
  • Modification: You can edit or delete any book entries at any time
  • Deletion: You can request account deletion by contacting us
  • Export: You can request a copy of your data

Cookies and Tracking

BookTracker uses minimal cookies only for authentication purposes. We do not use tracking cookies or analytics services.

Children's Privacy

BookTracker is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.
